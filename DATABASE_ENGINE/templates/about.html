Project Information


Welcome. This site is a place for exploring the past of the Oscars. With this site you can navigate through the years, the people, the movies, and the awards that have made the Oscars what they are today. This site is for all people looking to experience the history of the Oscars. It is meant to provide a simple way of navigating through the Osacars, so thank you for joining us and please enjoy.
